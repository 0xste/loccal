Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025

1. Introduction

At Loccal ("we", "our", or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website or mobile application (collectively, the "Platform").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our Platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not access or use our Platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information we collect automatically when you access or use our Platform, and information from third-party sources.

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us when you register for an account, create or modify your profile, express interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, or otherwise communicate with us. This information may include:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Login credentials for your account
  • Profile information, including preferences
  • Employment information (for employees using the platform)
  • Business information (for businesses offering perks)
  • Transaction and payment information
  • Feedback, correspondence, or other information you provide to us

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you access or use our Platform, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Location Information: With your consent, we collect precise location information from your mobile device to provide location-based services, such as showing you nearby perks.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our Platform, including hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your use of our Platform, such as the pages or content you view, the features you use, the actions you take, and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Platform and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect about you for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Platform
  • Process transactions and manage your account
  • Connect employees with relevant local business perks
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Develop new products and services
  • Personalize your experience on our Platform
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Platform
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information about you as follows or as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy:

  • With Local Businesses and Employers: We share necessary information to facilitate the connection between employees, employers, and local businesses for perks redemption.
  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors, service providers, and other business partners who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf.
  • In Response to Legal Process: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • For Protection of Rights: We may disclose information when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
  • In Connection with a Sale or Business Transaction: If we sell or transfer all or a portion of our business or assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with third parties when we have your consent to do so.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information we collect from you for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements, to resolve disputes, and to enforce our agreements.

6. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal information, transmission of personal information to and from our Platform is at your own risk.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following data protection rights:

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you
  • The right of rectification (to correct inaccurate information)
  • The right to object to the processing of your personal data
  • The right to restriction (requesting that we restrict the processing of your personal data)
  • The right to data portability (requesting a copy of your data in a structured format)
  • The right to withdraw consent

8. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
