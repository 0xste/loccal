About Loccal

We're building a platform that connects employees with local businesses to create a stronger, more vibrant community.

Our Story

Loccal was born out of a simple observation: employees were missing out on amazing local businesses, and those businesses were missing out on connecting with the thousands of employees right on their doorstep.

Our founder, Aidan, noticed that his colleagues were often unaware of the fantastic independent cafés, shops, and services just a short walk from their office. Meanwhile, those same local businesses were struggling to reach the very people who worked nearby.

Founded in 2023 in the North East of England, Loccal started as a small pilot program connecting just three local businesses with one office building. The response was overwhelming – employees loved discovering new places, and businesses saw real growth in foot traffic and loyalty.

Our Mission

To create thriving local communities by connecting employees with the businesses around them through meaningful perks and experiences.

Support Local

Help local businesses thrive by connecting them with employees in their area.

Build Community

Create meaningful connections between people and the places around them.

Sustainability

Reduce commuting and promote walkable neighborhoods by encouraging local engagement.
