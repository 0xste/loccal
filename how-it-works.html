Frequently Asked Questions

Is Loccal free for employees?

Yes, Loccal is completely free for employees. You can access all perks and offers without any subscription fees or charges.

How do businesses benefit from Loccal?

Businesses gain exposure to employees working nearby, increase foot traffic, and build customer loyalty. Our commission model means you only pay when customers redeem perks.

What types of perks can be offered?

Perks can include discounts, special offers, loyalty rewards, exclusive events, early access, and unique experiences tailored to your business and customer preferences.

How do employers measure the impact?

Employers receive detailed analytics on employee engagement, perk usage, satisfaction metrics, and estimated savings, helping measure ROI and community impact.

Where is Loccal currently available?

Loccal is currently available in the North East of England, focusing on Newcastle, Durham, and Gateshead. We're planning to expand to more regions soon!
