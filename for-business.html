How It Works For Businesses

1. Join the Platform

Sign up for Loccal and create your business profile. Tell us about your business, your busy and quiet periods, and what makes you special.

2. Create Your Perks

Design special offers or exclusive perks for employees. This could be discounts, loyalty rewards, special events, or unique experiences.

3. Connect With Employees

Your business and perks will be visible to employees using the Loccal platform who work in your area. They can browse, save, and redeem your offers.

4. Track Results & Optimize

Monitor the performance of your perks, see what's working, and adjust your offers to maximize results. We provide detailed analytics to help you succeed.
